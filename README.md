# DAS Intelligence Skills

**DAS Intelligence Skills** is a professional AI skills library for commerce, marketing, marketplaces, SEO, analytics, operations, logistics, legal workflows, and international business growth.

The project is designed as a structured knowledge and execution layer for AI assistants such as Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Qwen, Kimi, DeepSeek, Cursor, VS Code, OpenCode, and other agentic coding or business automation environments.

## Mission

Build a practical AI operating system for international e-commerce and business execution.

This repository will contain modular skills for:

- marketing strategy;
- website conversion optimization;
- SEO and content growth;
- analytics and performance diagnostics;
- market and customer research;
- sales and B2B export;
- operations and SOPs;
- logistics and fulfillment;
- legal and regulatory workflows;
- marketplace growth across Wildberries, Ozon, Amazon, Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop, Noon, and future platforms;
- high-level AI directors and specialist agents.

## Skill Standard

Each skill should be built as a professional module, not as a loose prompt.

Recommended structure:

```text
skill-name/
├── SKILL.md
├── README.md
├── metadata.yaml
├── references/
├── templates/
├── examples/
└── evals/
```
