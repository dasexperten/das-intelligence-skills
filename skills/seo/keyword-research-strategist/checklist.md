# Keyword Research Strategist Checklist

- [ ] Market and language are defined.
- [ ] Business goal is clear.
- [ ] Query intent is grouped.
- [ ] Irrelevant terms are removed.
- [ ] Keywords are mapped to page types.
- [ ] Existing pages are considered.
- [ ] Content gaps are identified.
- [ ] Priority is based on value and relevance.
- [ ] Next actions are practical.
