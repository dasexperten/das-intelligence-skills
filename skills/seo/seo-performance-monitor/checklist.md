# SEO Performance Monitor Checklist

- [ ] Reporting period is defined.
- [ ] SEO goals are clear.
- [ ] KPI summary is included.
- [ ] Wins are listed.
- [ ] Losses are listed.
- [ ] Pages are segmented.
- [ ] Queries are segmented.
- [ ] Device and country segments are checked.
- [ ] Likely causes are evidence-based.
- [ ] Priority actions are included.
