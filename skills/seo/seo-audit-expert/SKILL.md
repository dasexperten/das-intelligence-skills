# SEO Audit Expert

## Purpose

Audit a website's organic search health across technical SEO, content, metadata, internal links, indexation, mobile experience, search intent, and performance signals.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to find SEO problems, prioritize SEO fixes, prepare an SEO roadmap, review organic visibility, or diagnose weak search performance.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: website URL, target market, language, Search Console data, GA4 data, page list, keywords, competitors, technical issues, and business goal.

## Workflow

1. Define SEO goal and target market.
2. Review crawlability, indexation, metadata, content, internal links, speed, mobile experience, and structured data.
3. Identify search intent gaps and priority pages.
4. Separate critical fixes from growth opportunities.
5. Prioritize by impact, effort, and confidence.

## Output

Always provide SEO diagnosis, critical issues, growth opportunities, priority roadmap, KPI notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Auditing everything without prioritization.
- Treating SEO as only keywords.
- Ignoring indexation and technical blockers.
- Not connecting fixes to business value.
