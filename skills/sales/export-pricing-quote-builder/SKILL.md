# Export Pricing Quote Builder

## Purpose

Build clear export price and quote summaries for distributors, buyers, importers, and partners.

## When to Use

Use this skill when preparing price offers, MOQ notes, Incoterms notes, payment notes, sample cost notes, or quote comparisons.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: product list, country, currency, price, MOQ, cost, margin, shipping terms, payment terms, sample plan, and validity period.

## Workflow

1. Define quote purpose and buyer type.
2. List products, prices, MOQ, and currency.
3. Add shipping, payment, and validity notes.
4. Check margin and risk.
5. Prepare clean quote summary and next step.

## Output

Always provide quote summary, product table, terms notes, margin notes, risks, and next step.

## Common Mistakes

- Missing validity period.
- Mixing currencies.
- No MOQ.
- No margin check.
