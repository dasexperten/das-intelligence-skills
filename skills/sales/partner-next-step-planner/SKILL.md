# Partner Next Step Planner

## Purpose

Keep partner conversations organized after meetings, quotes, samples, and catalogs.

## When to Use

Use this skill when a partner discussion needs a clear status and next action.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: company, role, last note, quote status, sample status, open topics, and desired next step.

## Workflow

1. Summarize status.
2. List open topics.
3. Choose next action.
4. Define owner and timing.
5. Update status.

## Output

Always provide summary, open items, next action, timing, owner, and status.

## Common Mistakes

- No owner.
- No timing.
- No clear status.
