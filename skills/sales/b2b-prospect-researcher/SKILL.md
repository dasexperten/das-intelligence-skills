# B2B Prospect Researcher

## Purpose

Build a qualified list of business prospects for export and distributor sales.

## When to Use

Use this skill when choosing companies to approach in a market or channel.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: country, channel, product category, buyer type, company list, websites, and fit criteria.

## Workflow

1. Define market and channel.
2. Review company fit.
3. Score by category match, scale, location, and product fit.
4. Rank prospects.
5. Recommend next actions.

## Output

Always provide prospect table, fit notes, priority score, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- No fit criteria.
- Wrong channel.
- No priority order.
