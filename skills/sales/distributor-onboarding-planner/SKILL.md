# Distributor Onboarding Planner

## Purpose

Plan distributor onboarding so a new partner receives the right materials, product data, pricing, launch plan, reporting rhythm, and responsibilities.

## When to Use

Use this skill after choosing a distributor or partner and before first shipment or market launch.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: distributor, country, product list, pricing, launch channels, required documents, marketing materials, stock plan, reporting needs, and timeline.

## Workflow

1. Define onboarding goal and launch scope.
2. List required commercial, product, legal, logistics, and marketing materials.
3. Assign owners and deadlines.
4. Build launch checklist and reporting rhythm.
5. Identify risks before launch.

## Output

Always provide onboarding plan, material checklist, owner table, launch steps, reporting rhythm, risks, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Sending partner materials without context.
- No launch timeline.
- No owner for each task.
- No reporting rhythm.
