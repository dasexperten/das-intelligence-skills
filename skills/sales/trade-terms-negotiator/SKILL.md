# Trade Terms Negotiator

## Purpose

Prepare practical B2B trade terms for distributors, retailers, importers, and pharmacy channels.

## When to Use

Use this skill when discussing price, MOQ, payment timing, territory, exclusivity, marketing support, delivery terms, and performance conditions.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: buyer type, product, price list, MOQ, margin, payment terms, territory, exclusivity request, marketing budget, delivery terms, and target outcome.

## Workflow

1. Define business goal and minimum acceptable terms.
2. Separate fixed terms from flexible terms.
3. Identify risks and trade-offs.
4. Prepare counter options.
5. Recommend next position and approval notes.

## Output

Always provide term summary, fixed terms, flexible terms, risk notes, counter options, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Giving exclusivity without targets.
- No minimum acceptable terms.
- Ignoring margin.
- No written summary.
