# Buyer Meeting Prep

## Purpose

Prepare for buyer, distributor, importer, or retail meetings with a clear agenda, facts, questions, proof, and next step.

## When to Use

Use this skill before B2B calls, trade fair meetings, distributor meetings, retail buyer meetings, and follow-up negotiations.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: company, buyer role, country, product, meeting goal, previous messages, open questions, pricing notes, proof points, and concerns.

## Workflow

1. Define meeting goal and decision maker.
2. Summarize known context.
3. Prepare talking points and proof.
4. Prepare questions to ask.
5. Define likely outcomes and next steps.

## Output

Always provide meeting brief, agenda, talking points, questions, risks, and next step.

## Common Mistakes

- No meeting goal.
- No questions prepared.
- Too much product detail.
- No follow-up action.
