# Partner Fit Review

## Purpose

Review whether a possible partner fits the market, channel, product category, and cooperation goal.

## When to Use

Use this skill before sending materials, samples, quotes, or territory discussions.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: company, country, channel, role, product fit, references, requested cooperation, and next step.

## Workflow

1. Define cooperation goal.
2. Review channel and product fit.
3. Review company signals and references.
4. List missing information.
5. Recommend next step.

## Output

Always provide fit summary, missing information, review level, next step, and notes.

## Common Mistakes

- No fit criteria.
- No reference check.
- No written decision.
