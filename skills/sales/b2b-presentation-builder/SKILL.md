# B2B Presentation Builder

## Purpose

Build concise B2B presentations for buyers, distributors, retailers, pharmacy chains, and importers.

## When to Use

Use this skill when preparing a meeting opening, buyer deck, trade fair talk, or partner presentation.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: buyer type, product, market, channel, buyer need, proof points, offer, competitors, and desired next step.

## Workflow

1. Define buyer and meeting goal.
2. Identify buyer need and business benefit.
3. Choose proof points and product story.
4. Build short presentation and supporting points.
5. End with clear next step.

## Output

Always provide presentation summary, buyer need, value points, proof points, question notes, and next step.

## Common Mistakes

- Talking only about the brand.
- No buyer benefit.
- Too much detail.
- No next step.
