# Export Market Prospecting

## Purpose

Assess export markets and identify the most practical first channels, buyer types, and next steps.

## When to Use

Use this skill when choosing countries, comparing markets, planning distributor search, or preparing export expansion.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: country list, product category, target channel, price tier, competitors, regulations, logistics, duties, buyer types, and budget.

## Workflow

1. Define export goal and product category.
2. Compare market size, channel fit, competition, regulation, logistics, and price level.
3. Identify best entry path.
4. Recommend buyer types and first actions.
5. List risks and validation steps.

## Output

Always provide market comparison, entry path, buyer type list, risks, validation plan, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing country only by population.
- Ignoring regulation and logistics.
- No channel focus.
- No validation step.
