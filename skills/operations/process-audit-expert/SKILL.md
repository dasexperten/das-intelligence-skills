# Process Audit Expert

## Purpose

Review business processes to find bottlenecks, unclear owners, repeated mistakes, missing checks, and automation opportunities.

## When to Use

Use this skill when a workflow feels slow, messy, repeated, unclear, or dependent on one person.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: process name, steps, owner, tools, pain points, delays, errors, handoffs, reports, and desired result.

## Workflow

1. Map current process.
2. Identify bottlenecks, unclear roles, and repeated issues.
3. Separate must-fix issues from nice-to-have improvements.
4. Recommend simplified workflow, owners, and checks.
5. Add automation or reporting opportunities.

## Output

Always provide process diagnosis, bottlenecks, role gaps, fix list, automation ideas, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Fixing symptoms only.
- No owner clarity.
- No quality check.
- Adding automation before simplifying.
