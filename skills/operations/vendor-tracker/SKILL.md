# Vendor Tracker

## Purpose

Track suppliers, agencies, service providers, contractors, and operational partners in one clear system.

## When to Use

Use this skill when comparing vendors, following tasks, checking delivery status, or keeping partner information organized.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: vendor name, service, contact, cost, scope, deadline, current status, issues, documents, and next action.

## Workflow

1. List vendor and scope.
2. Capture contacts, costs, dates, and status.
3. Identify open issues and missing items.
4. Assign owner and next action.
5. Review status rhythm.

## Output

Always provide vendor table, status notes, open items, owner notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- No owner.
- No date.
- No scope.
- No status rhythm.
