# Director Decision Brief

## Purpose

Prepare concise decision briefs so management can choose between options with facts, tradeoffs, risks, and next actions.

## When to Use

Use this skill when a business choice must be made about price, channel, partner, market, supplier, budget, or priority.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: decision question, options, facts, numbers, risks, constraints, deadline, owner, and desired outcome.

## Workflow

1. Define decision question.
2. List options and key facts.
3. Compare impact, cost, effort, risk, and timing.
4. Recommend one option or next validation step.
5. Define owner and next action.

## Output

Always provide decision summary, option table, tradeoffs, risks, recommendation, and next action.

## Common Mistakes

- No decision question.
- Too many options.
- No risk notes.
- No recommendation.
