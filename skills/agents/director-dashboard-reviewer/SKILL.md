# Director Dashboard Reviewer

## Purpose

Review management dashboards and explain what matters, what changed, and what decision may be needed.

## When to Use

Use this skill when reviewing sales, marketing, marketplace, operations, finance, or project dashboards.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: dashboard screenshots, KPI table, date range, targets, prior period, business context, and decision need.

## Workflow

1. Define dashboard purpose and period.
2. Identify key changes and unusual signals.
3. Separate useful metrics from noise.
4. Explain likely meaning and decision needs.
5. Recommend next actions.

## Output

Always provide dashboard summary, key changes, risk notes, decision notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Reading every number.
- No comparison period.
- No decision link.
- No action list.
