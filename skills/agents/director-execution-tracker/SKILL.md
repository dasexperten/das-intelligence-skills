# Director Execution Tracker

## Purpose

Track execution of important business decisions, priorities, projects, and owner actions.

## When to Use

Use this skill when management needs to see whether important work is moving, finished, or needs attention.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: project, decision, owner, action list, dates, status, blockers, metric notes, and next step.

## Workflow

1. List active priorities.
2. Check owner, status, timing, and blockers.
3. Separate finished, active, delayed, and waiting items.
4. Recommend next management action.
5. Update tracker format.

## Output

Always provide execution summary, status table, delayed items, owner notes, metric notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- No owner.
- No due date.
- No status.
- No next management action.
