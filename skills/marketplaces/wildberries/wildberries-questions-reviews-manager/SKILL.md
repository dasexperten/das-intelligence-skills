# Wildberries Questions Reviews Manager

## Purpose

Manage Wildberries questions and reviews so answers build trust, remove doubts, and reveal card improvement opportunities.

## When to Use

Use this skill when answering WB customer questions, reviewing feedback, preparing response templates, or finding card gaps from questions and reviews.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: SKU, question, review, rating, product facts, card content, policy info, and repeated themes.

## Workflow

1. Identify the real customer concern.
2. Answer directly using verified product facts.
3. Keep tone calm and useful.
4. Note repeated themes and missing card information.
5. Recommend card, image, or FAQ updates.

## Output

Always provide answer draft, concern, fact used, content gap, update idea, and next action.

## Common Mistakes

- Vague answers.
- Unsupported claims.
- Defensive tone.
- Ignoring repeated questions.
