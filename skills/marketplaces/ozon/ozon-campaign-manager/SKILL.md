# Ozon Campaign Manager

## Purpose

Plan Ozon campaigns so budget, bids, discounts, visibility, ranking goals, and margin are controlled.

## When to Use

Use this skill when planning Ozon ads, search campaigns, promotions, launch pushes, or cleanup of weak campaigns.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: SKU, category, budget, CPC, conversion, orders, revenue, stock, price, margin, promotion calendar, and card readiness.

## Workflow

1. Define campaign goal.
2. Check card readiness, stock, and price.
3. Review budget, CPC, conversion, and economics.
4. Plan campaign structure and timing.
5. Set KPIs, stop rules, and next actions.

## Output

Always provide campaign diagnosis, SKU priority, budget logic, calendar plan, KPI notes, risks, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Campaigns before content is ready.
- No stop rule.
- Ignoring stock.
- Ignoring margin.
