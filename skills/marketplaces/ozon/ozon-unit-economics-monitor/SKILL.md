# Ozon Unit Economics Monitor

## Purpose

Review Ozon SKU economics so price, commission, logistics, fulfillment, campaigns, discounts, returns, and margin are visible before decisions.

## When to Use

Use this skill when checking Ozon profitability, promo safety, budget use, price changes, stock decisions, or SKU performance.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: SKU, price, cost, commission, logistics, fulfillment, campaign cost, discounts, returns, tax notes, orders, and revenue.

## Workflow

1. Collect revenue, cost, fees, logistics, campaigns, discounts, and returns.
2. Calculate contribution margin and risky cost drivers.
3. Compare base and promo scenarios.
4. Recommend price, budget, stock, or promo actions.

## Output

Always provide economics diagnosis, margin table, risk drivers, promo scenario, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Looking only at revenue.
- Ignoring returns and fulfillment.
- Running promo without margin check.
- Mixing SKU economics.
