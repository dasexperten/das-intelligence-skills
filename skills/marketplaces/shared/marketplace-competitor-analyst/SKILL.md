# Marketplace Competitor Analyst

## Purpose

Analyze marketplace competitors to identify pricing gaps, content gaps, ranking signals, visual patterns, review insights, offer differences, and opportunities to improve product performance.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to compare competitor cards, understand category leaders, prepare a launch, improve listing content, or find differentiation opportunities.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, category, product, competitor links or screenshots, prices, reviews, ratings, visuals, titles, attributes, promotions, ads visibility, and ranking position.

## Workflow

1. Identify direct competitors and category leaders.
2. Compare title, attributes, content, images, price, reviews, promotions, and proof.
3. Extract competitor strengths and weak spots.
4. Identify shopper language and objections.
5. Recommend positioning, listing, pricing, media, and promotion actions.

## Output

Always provide competitor map, gap analysis, pricing context, content gaps, review insights, differentiation ideas, and priority actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Comparing with irrelevant competitors.
- Copying competitors without strategy.
- Ignoring review insights.
- Looking only at price.
- Missing visual and proof gaps.
