# Marketplace Analytics Expert Checklist

- [ ] Business question is clear.
- [ ] Date range is defined.
- [ ] SKU data is segmented.
- [ ] Visibility metrics are reviewed.
- [ ] Click and conversion metrics are reviewed.
- [ ] Ads data is separated from organic where possible.
- [ ] Stock and price are considered.
- [ ] Bottleneck is identified.
- [ ] Priority actions are tied to KPIs.
