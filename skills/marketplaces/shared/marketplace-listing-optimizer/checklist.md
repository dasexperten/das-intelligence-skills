# Marketplace Listing Optimizer Checklist

- [ ] Marketplace and category are clear.
- [ ] Buyer intent is identified.
- [ ] Title is readable and search-aware.
- [ ] Benefits are clear.
- [ ] Attributes are complete.
- [ ] Claims are supported by proof.
- [ ] Images and text work together.
- [ ] Reviews and questions are considered.
- [ ] Objections are addressed.
- [ ] Priority actions are practical.
