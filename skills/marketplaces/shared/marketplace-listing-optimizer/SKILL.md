# Marketplace Listing Optimizer

## Purpose

Improve marketplace product listings so shoppers understand the product, trust the claims, compare options, and move toward purchase.

Use this skill across marketplaces such as Wildberries, Ozon, Amazon, Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop, Noon, and other commerce platforms.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to:

- improve a marketplace card or listing;
- rewrite product title, bullets, description, or attributes;
- clarify benefits and proof;
- reduce buyer objections;
- prepare a listing for launch or relaunch;
- adapt product content for a specific marketplace.

## Inputs

Useful inputs:

- marketplace name;
- product name and category;
- current listing text;
- product features and benefits;
- images or media notes;
- reviews and objections;
- competitor listings;
- SEO keywords;
- pricing and offer context.

## Workflow

1. Identify marketplace, category, and buyer intent.
2. Review title, attributes, description, media, proof, and CTA logic.
3. Translate features into shopper benefits.
4. Identify missing information and objections.
5. Recommend optimized title, bullets, description, attributes, and content blocks.
6. Adapt recommendations to marketplace rules and search behavior.
7. Prioritize fixes by sales impact.

## Output

Always provide:

- listing diagnosis;
- optimized title logic;
- benefit hierarchy;
- attribute and content fixes;
- proof and trust improvements;
- objection handling;
- keyword notes;
- priority actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Copying website copy into marketplace listing.
- Overloading title with unreadable keywords.
- Listing features without benefits.
- Making claims without proof.
- Ignoring reviews and questions.
