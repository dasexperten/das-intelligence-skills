# Marketplace Account Health Monitor

## Purpose

Monitor marketplace account health signals that may affect selling ability, visibility, delivery quality, customer trust, and operational stability.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to review account risks, operational warnings, fulfillment issues, policy problems, cancellation risk, late delivery, content moderation, or support metrics.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, account warnings, fulfillment metrics, cancellation data, return data, moderation notices, stock issues, customer complaints, and seller performance data.

## Workflow

1. Identify the account health signals available.
2. Separate critical risks from routine warnings.
3. Connect operational issues to sales and visibility risk.
4. Recommend fixes, owners, and timing.
5. Create monitoring rhythm and escalation rules.

## Output

Always provide account health diagnosis, risk list, likely causes, priority fixes, escalation notes, and monitoring checklist.

## Common Mistakes

- Ignoring small warnings until they become restrictions.
- Treating account health as only support work.
- Not connecting fulfillment quality to sales performance.
- No owner for fixes.
