# Marketplace Promotion Planner

## Purpose

Plan marketplace promotions that increase sales, ranking, visibility, and stock movement while protecting margin and avoiding waste.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to prepare promotions, sale campaigns, discounts, coupons, bundles, flash deals, or seasonal marketplace activity.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, product, current price, cost, margin, stock, promotion mechanics, calendar, competitor promos, sales goal, and ad budget.

## Workflow

1. Define promotion goal: launch, ranking, stock movement, profit, or visibility.
2. Review margin, stock, price, and platform mechanics.
3. Choose promotion type and timing.
4. Connect promotion with listing, ads, and ranking actions.
5. Define KPI, guardrails, and stop rules.

## Output

Always provide promotion diagnosis, campaign plan, price logic, budget notes, margin guardrails, KPI table, risks, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Discounting without a goal.
- Ignoring margin.
- Promoting weak listings.
- Not aligning ads with promotion.
- No stop rule.
