# TikTok Shop Listing Video Optimizer

## Purpose

Improve TikTok Shop product listings and videos so the offer is clear, mobile-first, creator-friendly, and ready for short-form conversion.

## When to Use

Use this skill when improving TikTok Shop titles, product content, product images, short videos, hooks, captions, and shopping flow.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: product, category, current listing, videos, creator content, audience, price, reviews, questions, offer, and platform rules.

## Workflow

1. Review listing clarity, media, hook, proof, and buying path.
2. Identify missing product facts and trust elements.
3. Recommend title, content, video, caption, and CTA improvements.
4. Prioritize mobile-first fixes.

## Output

Always provide listing diagnosis, video hook plan, content fixes, proof notes, CTA notes, and priority actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Listing looks like a normal catalog page.
- Slow video opening.
- No clear benefit.
- Weak trust signals.
