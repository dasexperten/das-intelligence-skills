# TikTok Shop Market Localization

## Purpose

Prepare TikTok Shop listings and content for local market fit, platform rules, language clarity, product facts, and buyer trust.

## When to Use

Use this skill when adapting listings, videos, creator briefs, offers, shipping notes, or product statements for a TikTok Shop market.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: target market, product, listing copy, video copy, product facts, ingredients or specs, shipping notes, return notes, language, and platform rules.

## Workflow

1. Define target market and language.
2. Review product facts, offer wording, shipping, and return notes.
3. Identify wording risks and missing trust details.
4. Recommend localized copy and content changes.
5. Add approval and validation steps.

## Output

Always provide localization diagnosis, product fact notes, copy fixes, trust gaps, market notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Direct translation without local intent.
- Weak proof.
- Missing delivery or return clarity.
- Ignoring platform rules.
