# Stock Planner

## Purpose

Plan product stock so goods do not run out, overstock, or arrive too late.

## When to Use

Use this skill when planning purchase orders, warehouse stock, marketplace stock, safety stock, or reorder timing.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: SKU, current stock, sales speed, lead time, minimum stock, shipment time, seasonality, planned campaigns, and order quantity.

## Workflow

1. Review current stock and sales speed.
2. Estimate lead time and reorder point.
3. Check planned campaigns and seasonality.
4. Identify low stock and overstock risk.
5. Recommend order quantity and timing.

## Output

Always provide stock diagnosis, reorder point, order quantity, timing notes, risk notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Ordering too late.
- Ignoring lead time.
- Ignoring campaigns.
- No safety stock.
