# Third Party Logistics Evaluator

## Purpose

Evaluate warehouse and fulfillment partners by service scope, price, location, integration, reporting, reliability, and fit.

## When to Use

Use this skill when comparing 3PLs, warehouses, fulfillment providers, prep centers, or cross-border logistics partners.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: provider list, country, service scope, rates, minimums, warehouse location, system integration, reporting, SLA, and references.

## Workflow

1. Define logistics need and market.
2. Compare service scope, cost, location, integration, and reliability.
3. Identify hidden costs and missing details.
4. Score providers.
5. Recommend shortlist and test plan.

## Output

Always provide provider comparison, score table, hidden cost notes, missing information, shortlist, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing only by storage price.
- Ignoring minimum fees.
- No test shipment.
- No reporting requirement.
