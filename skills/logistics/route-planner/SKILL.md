# Route Planner

## Purpose

Compare shipping routes and choose practical options by cost, speed, reliability, documents, and risk.

## When to Use

Use this skill when comparing air, sea, rail, courier, bonded, or warehouse routes between countries.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: origin, destination, product, quantity, weight, volume, target date, budget, route options, carriers, and constraints.

## Workflow

1. Define shipment goal.
2. List route options.
3. Compare cost, speed, reliability, documents, and risk.
4. Identify bottlenecks and missing data.
5. Recommend route and next actions.

## Output

Always provide route comparison, recommended route, cost notes, timing notes, document notes, risks, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing only by price.
- Ignoring documents.
- Ignoring storage and handoff points.
- No backup route.
