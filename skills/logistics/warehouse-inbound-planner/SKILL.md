# Warehouse Inbound Planner

## Purpose

Plan inbound delivery to a warehouse or fulfillment center so labels, cartons, timing, documents, and receiving rules are ready.

## When to Use

Use this skill before sending goods to a 3PL, marketplace warehouse, prep center, or local stock point.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: warehouse, product list, carton count, SKU list, labels, pallet rules, delivery date, carrier, receiving rules, and documents.

## Workflow

1. Define warehouse and receiving rules.
2. Check SKU, carton, label, and pallet requirements.
3. Prepare delivery timing and carrier handoff.
4. Identify missing documents or labels.
5. Create inbound checklist and next steps.

## Output

Always provide inbound plan, SKU table, carton notes, label notes, document notes, timing, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Missing labels.
- Mixed SKUs without rules.
- No delivery booking.
- No carton count.
