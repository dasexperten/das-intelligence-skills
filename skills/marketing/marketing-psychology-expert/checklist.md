# Marketing Psychology Expert Checklist

- [ ] Desired customer action is clear.
- [ ] Main desire is identified.
- [ ] Main doubt or fear is identified.
- [ ] Trust barriers are named.
- [ ] Rational and emotional drivers are separated.
- [ ] Claims are supported by proof.
- [ ] Message avoids manipulation.
- [ ] Urgency has a real reason.
- [ ] Recommended tests are practical.
- [ ] Output improves clarity, trust, or motivation.
