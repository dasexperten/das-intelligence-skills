# Product Marketing Expert Checklist

- [ ] Product and market are clear.
- [ ] Target customer is specific.
- [ ] Customer problem is identified.
- [ ] Desired outcome is clear.
- [ ] Features are translated into benefits.
- [ ] Proof points support claims.
- [ ] Objections are addressed.
- [ ] Channel messages are adapted.
- [ ] Launch or optimization actions are practical.
- [ ] Messaging is clear and supportable.
