# Marketing Planner Checklist

- [ ] Business objective is clear.
- [ ] Time horizon is defined.
- [ ] Target audience is specific.
- [ ] Positioning and offer are summarized.
- [ ] Channels are prioritized.
- [ ] Actions are grouped by acquisition, conversion, and retention.
- [ ] KPIs are tied to actions.
- [ ] Timeline is practical.
- [ ] Risks and dependencies are named.
- [ ] Next steps are concrete.
