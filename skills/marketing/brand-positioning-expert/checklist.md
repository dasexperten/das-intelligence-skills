# Brand Positioning Expert Checklist

- [ ] Category frame is clear.
- [ ] Target customer is specific.
- [ ] Main alternatives are named.
- [ ] Differentiation is believable.
- [ ] Proof points support the claim.
- [ ] Positioning is simple enough to repeat.
- [ ] Website, marketplace, ads, and B2B messages are aligned.
- [ ] Weak claims and proof gaps are flagged.
- [ ] Next actions are practical.
