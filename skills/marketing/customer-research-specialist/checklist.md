# Customer Research Specialist Checklist

- [ ] Research goal is clear.
- [ ] Data source is named.
- [ ] Customer segments are separated.
- [ ] Needs and doubts are grouped.
- [ ] Repeated customer language is captured.
- [ ] Purchase triggers are identified.
- [ ] Objections are converted into message ideas.
- [ ] Insights are not based on one isolated comment.
- [ ] Recommended tests are practical.
