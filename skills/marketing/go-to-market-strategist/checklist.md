# Go-To-Market Strategist Checklist

- [ ] Launch goal is clear.
- [ ] Success metric is defined.
- [ ] Target market and customer are specific.
- [ ] Positioning and offer are summarized.
- [ ] Channel sequence is realistic.
- [ ] Required assets are listed.
- [ ] Operational dependencies are named.
- [ ] 30/60/90-day roadmap is practical.
- [ ] KPIs cover awareness, conversion, and readiness.
- [ ] Risks and fallback actions are included.
