# Brand Strategy Expert Benchmark

## Test Case 1 — Generic Brand Audit

### Input

Audit the brand strategy for an oral care company selling toothpaste and toothbrushes through marketplaces and an international website.

### Expected Output

The answer should include:

- category diagnosis;
- target audience;
- positioning statement;
- brand pillars;
- proof points;
- channel translation;
- priority actions.

## Test Case 2 — Marketplace Brand Problem

### Input

The brand sells well on marketplaces but has weak recognition outside the marketplace. Build a strategy to strengthen brand memory.

### Expected Output

The answer should include:

- marketplace dependency risk;
- memory assets;
- packaging and visual consistency;
- review mining;
- content strategy;
- website trust-building;
- retargeting and repeat purchase actions.

## Evaluation Criteria

Score from 1 to 5:

1. Specificity
2. Strategic clarity
3. Channel practicality
4. Quality of differentiation
5. Actionability
