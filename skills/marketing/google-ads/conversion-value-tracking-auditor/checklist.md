# Conversion Value Tracking Auditor Checklist

- [ ] Every conversion action is listed.
- [ ] Primary and secondary goals are separated.
- [ ] Values and currency are correct.
- [ ] Duplicate purchase or lead signals are checked.
- [ ] Transaction IDs are used where relevant.
- [ ] GA4 and Ads samples are compared.
- [ ] CRM or offline imports are reviewed.
- [ ] End-to-end tests are complete.
