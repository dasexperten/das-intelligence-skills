# Google Ads CPC Quality Score Optimizer Checklist

- [ ] CPC is reviewed with CPA and value.
- [ ] Search terms and match types are segmented.
- [ ] Expected CTR is reviewed.
- [ ] Ad relevance is reviewed.
- [ ] Landing-page experience is reviewed.
- [ ] Expensive irrelevant traffic is identified.
- [ ] Intent groups are tightened.
- [ ] Bid changes follow relevance fixes.
