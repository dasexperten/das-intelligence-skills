# Community Marketing Expert Checklist

- [ ] Community purpose is clear.
- [ ] Business goal is defined.
- [ ] Target members are specific.
- [ ] Shared motivation is strong.
- [ ] Platform choice fits the audience.
- [ ] Content pillars are practical.
- [ ] Engagement rituals are repeatable.
- [ ] Member roles are defined.
- [ ] Moderation rules are included.
- [ ] KPIs measure participation quality.
- [ ] Launch plan is realistic.
