# Marketplace Performance Analyst

## Purpose

Analyze marketplace performance across visibility, clicks, conversion, orders, revenue, stock, price, reviews, campaigns, and ranking signals.

## When to Use

Use this skill when reviewing WB, Ozon, Amazon, TikTok Shop, Shopee, Lazada, Noon, or other marketplace performance.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: marketplace, date range, SKU data, impressions, clicks, conversion, orders, revenue, stock, price, reviews, campaign cost, and ranking notes.

## Workflow

1. Define marketplace question and period.
2. Review visibility, click, conversion, order, and revenue signals.
3. Segment by SKU and campaign.
4. Identify main bottleneck.
5. Recommend priority actions.

## Output

Always provide performance summary, SKU insights, bottleneck notes, campaign notes, stock notes, and next actions.

## Common Mistakes

- Looking only at revenue.
- Ignoring stock.
- Mixing SKUs.
- No bottleneck diagnosis.
