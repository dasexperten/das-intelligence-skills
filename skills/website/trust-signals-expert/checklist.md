# Trust Signals Expert Checklist

- [ ] Visitor trust concerns are identified.
- [ ] Claims are supported with proof.
- [ ] Reviews or testimonials are visible.
- [ ] Return and delivery policies are easy to find.
- [ ] Payment and security signals are visible.
- [ ] Company/contact information is clear.
- [ ] Certifications are shown accurately.
- [ ] Guarantees are explained simply.
- [ ] Trust signals are placed near decisions.
- [ ] Priority fixes are practical.
