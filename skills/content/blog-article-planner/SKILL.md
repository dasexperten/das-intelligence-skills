# Blog Article Planner

## Purpose

Plan useful blog articles that answer real questions, support SEO, build trust, and connect readers to products, offers, or next steps.

## When to Use

Use this skill when the task is to plan an article, guide, comparison post, expert post, FAQ article, or blog content roadmap.

## Inputs

Useful inputs: topic, audience, search intent, target questions, product facts, proof points, competitors, internal links, and CTA.

## Workflow

1. Define article goal and reader intent.
2. Choose article angle and title logic.
3. Build outline with useful sections.
4. Add proof, examples, FAQs, links, and CTA.
5. Create quality requirements and next actions.

## Output

Always provide article angle, outline, key points, proof needs, FAQ ideas, internal links, CTA, and next steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Writing an article with no reader goal.
- Weak outline.
- No proof.
- No next action.
